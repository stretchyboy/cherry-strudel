About This Project

Cherry Strudel makes it easy to bring existing musical notation into the Strudel live coding environment. Whether you have ABC files from traditional music archives or MIDI files from a digital audio workstation, these converters help you transform them into editable Strudel patterns.

Built with abcjs and @tonejs/midi.
